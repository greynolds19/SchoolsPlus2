About the Map

Schools are represented by circular markers. A bold outline symbolizes a cluster recruited school school. Explore characterisics of schools in different cities by zooming with the zoom keys in the upper-right corner, or by holding shift and dragging a box over an area.

Poverty Scale is median household income as a ratio of the national poverty threshold, $22,315.

All US Census data is from 2010.
